Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Best Friend ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, and pet details (name, breed, birthday, photos). We also collect usage data such as quiz responses, task completion history, and health records you choose to log.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide personalised breed recommendations, generate daily care tips based on your pet's breed, track health records and milestones, send care reminders (if enabled), and improve our services.

Data Storage and Security

Your account data is securely managed by Clerk (authentication). Your pet and app data is stored on Convex (database). All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Clerk for authentication, Convex for data storage, RevenueCat for subscription management, Dog CEO API and TheCatAPI for breed images. Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data through the app settings. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@bestfriendapp.com.

Children's Privacy

Best Friend is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@bestfriendapp.com.
